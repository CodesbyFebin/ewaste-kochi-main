Thinking about what to do with your old laptop in Kochi? The decision between **selling vs recycling** can significantly impact your wallet and the environment. Let's break down which option truly pays more in 2026.

## Quick Answer: Which Pays More?

**Selling your laptop typically pays more** if it's in working condition, but recycling offers better value for non-working devices and provides complete peace of mind with data security.

### **Selling Pays More When:**
- Laptop is in working condition
- Recent model (last 3-4 years)
- Good physical condition
- Has original accessories
- You want maximum cash immediately

### **Recycling Pays More When:**
- Laptop is non-working or damaged
- Data security is your priority
- You want environmental responsibility
- Laptop is very old (5+ years)
- You need compliance documentation
